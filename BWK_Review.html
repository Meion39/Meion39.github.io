Black Myth: Wukong

Black Myth: Wukong is an upcoming action role-playing game developed by the Chinese indie game studio Game Science. Announced in August 2020, the game is based on the classic Chinese novel "Journey to the West" and follows the adventures of Sun Wukong, the Monkey King. The game features a rich narrative, stunning visuals, and intense combat mechanics, drawing inspiration from titles like Dark Souls and God of War. Players will explore a mythical world filled with various creatures and challenges as they uncover the story of Sun Wukong and his quest for redemption.

The Good

  • Visuals are Unbelievable: This is the first thing everyone talks about. The game looks absolutely stunning. The environments, the character models, and especially the monster designs are top-tier, pushing the limits of what games look like. It perfectly captures the mythical scale of the Journey to the West.
  • Combat is Deep and Demanding: The fighting feels heavy and precise, very similar to games like Sekiro or Dark Souls. Wukong's staff combat has a great rhythm, and the various transformations and spells add a ton of variety. It’s challenging, but every difficult boss fight you win feels extremely satisfying.
  • Transformations are a Blast: The ability to transform into different creatures (like the Golden Cicada) isn't just a gimmick—it's key to solving puzzles and changing up combat tactics. These powers are creative and make the gameplay feel fresh throughout the whole adventure.

The Bad

  • Map Design Needs Work: While the world is beautiful, the actual map design could be significantly improved. The exploration often feels restricted due to too many invisible walls (air walls) that arbitrarily block paths, making the environment feel less open and the overall experience less fluid than expected for an adventure game of this scale.
  • Storytelling Can Be Dense: The lore is massive and heavily based on classic Chinese mythology. For players new to the "Journey to the West" story, some plot points and character motivations might feel a little confusing or hard to follow at first.

Final Thoughts

"Black Myth: Wukong is an absolute must-play for fans of demanding action RPGs." It delivers on its promise of being a beautiful, challenging, and epic adventure. If you love games that make you work for the win and appreciate incredible visual artistry, jump right into Wukong's journey.
