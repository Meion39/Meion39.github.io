Monster Hunter World:Iceborne

Monster Hunter World: Iceborne is an expansion pack for the action role-playing game Monster Hunter: World, developed and published by Capcom. Released in September 2019 for PlayStation 4 and Xbox One, and in January 2020 for Microsoft Windows, Iceborne introduces a new storyline, a new region called Hoarfrost Reach, and a plethora of new monsters, weapons, and armor sets.

The Good

  • Huge Expansion: Iceborne is more than just a DLC; it's a full sequel packed into an expansion. It adds a massive new snowy area (Hoarfrost Reach), a whole cast of challenging new monsters, and the highly difficult Master Rank difficulty level.
  • Clutch Claw is a Game Changer: The new Clutch Claw mechanic drastically changes combat. It allows you to grapple onto monsters, tenderize their hides to deal more damage, or slam them into walls for a knockdown. It adds verticality and speed to every fight, making combat even more dynamic and strategic.
  • Excellent Monster Design: The new flagship monster, Velkhana, is fantastic, and the returning fan-favorites (like the Zinogre) and new Elder Dragons provide some of the toughest and most rewarding hunts in the entire series.

The Bad

  • A Steep Learning Curve: Master Rank is very difficult. Even experienced World players will face a steep jump in difficulty. Monsters hit much harder and faster, and new players might find the grind to upgrade armor quite frustrating.
  • Tenderizing Can Feel Required: While the Clutch Claw is fun, the damage difference between tenderized and non-tenderized parts is so huge that it often feels like you must use the mechanic to be efficient, which can interrupt the flow of the classic combat loop.

Final Thoughts

"Iceborne is the definitive version of Monster Hunter: World." It takes an already great game and adds mountains of content, new mechanics, and ultimate challenges. If you enjoyed the base game, this expansion will consume hundreds more hours of your life with rewarding, action-packed hunts.
