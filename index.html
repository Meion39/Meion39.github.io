    Favorite Games

    My favorite games share a common thread: they offer a perfect blend of unrestricted freedom and masterful challenge. I’m drawn to worlds where I can forge my own path, whether that's through deep customization or pure skill. This passion is reflected in my top picks: the endless creativity and exploration of Terraria, the satisfying grind and epic monster battles of Monster Hunter, the breathtaking world design and puzzle-solving in The Legend of Zelda series, the chaotic, open-world storytelling of Grand Theft Auto (GTA), and the roguelike mastery and replayability of The Binding of Isaac. These titles aren't just games—they're deep rabbit holes of discovery.
